Your Outcomes

You'll be introduced to the business of sports from a marketing and sponsorship perspective and will develop the skills and knowledge base for careers in the sports and marketing industries.

Marketing skills are needed across the sport and recreation industries. The sports field isn’t like a traditional business and neither are the marketing & management skills needed to succeed in this area. With the large number of professional athletes, sports organizations, facilities and events all around the world, people with specialist sports marketing and business skills are in high demand.

Apply, in a research-informed manner, a range of marketing strategies and tactics to the practice of contemporary sports marketing.

Interpret and critically evaluate the marketing challenges faced by sports organisations operating in the global marketplace.

Critically evaluate a number of marketing theories, strategies and tactics in the 21st century sports context.
